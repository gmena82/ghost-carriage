Founded by trained security professionals who recognized a critical gap in the market. Ghost Carriage exists because executive safety cannot be an afterthought.

Mission

Failure Is Not an Option

"To allow each client and their family to freely exercise their normal way of life with minimal interruption — while safety and security remain at the highest level."

Ghost Carriage was founded by Shelby DuBois and Mark Allen, two professionals who saw the same problem in every city they operated in: executives settling for luxury transportation with zero tactical capability.

A chauffeured Escalade looks impressive. But the moment a credible threat emerges, an unarmed civilian driver is a liability — not an asset. We built Ghost Carriage to eliminate that gap entirely.

Our operators are not drivers who carry firearms. They are trained executive protection professionals who also drive. That distinction makes every difference.

Operating Principles

What Drives Every Decision

Proactive Threat Assessment

We conduct in-depth advance work, intelligence gathering, and background analysis before any engagement. We identify threats before they become incidents.

Encrypted Communications

All operational communications are encrypted. Client information, travel itineraries, and meeting schedules are never transmitted on unsecured channels.

NDA-Backed Confidentiality

Every engagement is governed by a mutual non-disclosure agreement. Your identity, movements, and the fact you used our services remain strictly confidential.

24/7 Operational Readiness

Threats do not operate on business hours. Neither do we. Our team maintains round-the-clock readiness for both preplanned and emergency engagements.

Low-Profile Operations

True security is invisible. Our operators blend into the executive environment seamlessly, drawing no attention while maintaining total awareness.

Compliance & Accountability

All armed operations are conducted under strict legal compliance. Every operator holds valid licensure, and all engagements adhere to federal and state regulations.

Operational Framework

How Ghost Carriage Operates

1

Secure Intake & NDA Execution

Initial contact is made through encrypted channels. Client identity and engagement details are secured under NDA before any planning begins.

2

Advance Work & Threat Assessment

Our team conducts pre-engagement reconnaissance: route analysis, venue assessment, background checks on key contacts, and threat probability mapping.

3

Mission Briefing & Deployment

Operators receive full mission briefs including contingency protocols, primary and alternate routes, communication checkpoints, and emergency escalation procedures.

4

Execution & Real-Time Monitoring

Throughout the engagement, GPS tracking, encrypted communications, and real-time threat monitoring keep every movement accountable and secure.

5

After-Action & Client Debrief

Post-engagement, our team documents observations and provides clients with a secure debrief on any notable threat intelligence gathered during the operation.
