Client Experience

Client Experience Lead

Her background in building service-based businesses taught her a simple truth: people remember how an experience feels long after it ends. Transportation plays a bigger role in that feeling than most companies realize.

Shelby Focuses On
  • ✦ Client communication and scheduling

Fleet & Operations

Meet Mark

Founder & Operations Director

Mark leads fleet strategy and operational execution. His focus is making sure every vehicle performs exactly as expected, every time.

This Includes

The Decision

Why We Built an All-Electric Fleet

Ghost Carriage is currently the only black car service in Kansas City operating a fully electric luxury fleet, including the Cadillac Escalade IQ and Cadillac Vistiq. This was not done for trend value. It was done because electric vehicles genuinely improve the rider experience.

Quieter Inside

No engine noise means you can take calls, have conversations, or simply decompress in genuine silence.

Smoother Acceleration

Electric motors deliver instant, seamless power. No shifts. No hesitation. Just smooth, controlled movement through city traffic.

Aligned with Modern Standards

More comfortable in city traffic, more aligned with how modern companies prefer to travel. Choosing electric allowed us to start fresh.
