Executive Protection:
The Proactive Standard

Bodyguards are reactive. Ghost Carriage Executive Protection (EP) agents are proactive. We utilize in-depth planning, advance work, and real-time intelligence to keep clients out of harm's way — before a threat ever emerges.

Methodology

The EP Operational Framework

Every Ghost Carriage EP engagement follows a structured, intelligence-driven protocol.

Intelligence Gathering

Open-source intelligence (OSINT), background checks on key contacts, and environmental threat mapping before any deployment.

Advance Work

Pre-site visits, venue assessments, and choke-point identification. We walk the route before you do so we can walk it safely with you.

Threat Assessment

Systematic evaluation of credible threats relevant to your specific profile, travel patterns, and public exposure level.

Encrypted Comms

All mission communications are encrypted. No itinerary details, locations, or client data transmitted on unsecured channels.

Evasive Driving

Operators are trained in defensive and evasive driving protocols. Primary and alternate routes are pre-planned for every engagement.

Contingency Protocols

Every engagement is mapped with contingency response plans. If conditions change mid-mission, the team adapts instantly without client disruption.

Ideal Clients

Who Requires Executive Protection

C-Suite Executives

CEOs, CFOs, and corporate officers whose schedules, assets, and families represent high-value targets.

International VIPs & Dignitaries

Foreign nationals and high-profile visitors unfamiliar with the local threat landscape, particularly during major events.

Political Figures

Elected officials and political operatives who require secure ground transportation outside of official details.

HNW Families

High-net-worth individuals and their immediate family members requiring secure daily mobility without overt security presence.
