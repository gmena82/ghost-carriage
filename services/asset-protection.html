The Method

Low-Profile is the
Highest Form of Security

The most dangerous moment for any high-value asset is its movement. Conspicuous armored vehicles advertise their contents. Ghost Carriage does the opposite — deploying vehicles that cannot be identified, tracked, or anticipated.

Our asset protection operations utilize unmarked, leased vehicles that hold no registration connection to Ghost Carriage, the client, or the asset being transported. When a vehicle cannot be identified, it cannot be targeted.

For high-risk valuables, we deploy Security Augmentation Teams (SAT) — additional armed operators who accompany the primary vehicle, providing overwatch, counter-surveillance, and rapid response capability throughout the transit.

Advanced Capability

Security Augmentation
Teams (SAT)

For assets requiring the highest level of protection, Ghost Carriage deploys Security Augmentation Teams alongside the primary transport vehicle. SAT units provide:

Counter-Surveillance

A secondary unit monitors the transport environment for surveillance, tailing vehicles, or unusual activity patterns.

Overwatch & Flanking

SAT operators position around the primary vehicle to provide 360-degree situational awareness and immediate response capability.

Rapid Response

If a threat materializes, the SAT can respond and neutralize while the primary vehicle executes an extraction protocol.

Chain of Custody

Documented handoff procedures ensure legal chain of custody is maintained from pickup through final delivery.
