The Platform

The 2023+ Cadillac Escalade:
Security on Wheels

Ghost Carriage does not operate armored vehicles. We operate something more sophisticated: elite armed professionals in ultra-low-profile executive SUVs, designed to be invisible, untrackable, and decisively capable if required.

Critical Note: Ghost Carriage vehicles are explicitly NON-armored. Our security model is built on concealment, speed, and operator capability — not static vehicle hardening. A vehicle that cannot be identified as a security asset cannot be targeted as one.

Onboard Capability

What the Vehicle Brings to the Mission

Real-Time GPS Tracking

Continuous location data transmitted to the Ghost Carriage operations team. Every movement is logged and monitored in real-time, providing immediate response capability if the vehicle deviates from its planned route.

Encrypted Comms System

All operator-to-ops communications are conducted on encrypted channels. Client conversations within the vehicle are never recorded or transmitted.

Panic Response Integration

A discreet panic response system allows either the client or operator to trigger immediate escalation protocols. Response assets are pre-positioned before each engagement.

Evasive Driving Capability

All Ghost Carriage operators are trained in defensive and evasive driving. The Escalade's engine and handling profile are well-suited for rapid acceleration and controlled evasion maneuvers.

Off-Grid Booking Available

For clients requiring maximum operational security, vehicles can be booked and deployed through encrypted channels with no digital trail connecting the client to the service.

Armed Operator Protocol

Every Ghost Carriage driver is a trained EP operator carrying a licensed firearm. The vehicle's security capability lives in the operator — not the metal surrounding the client.
